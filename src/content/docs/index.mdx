As a platform grows and new opportunities are explored, a pattern typically emerges; every new project and every new hire introduces friction to the product development process: slowing down development, increasing costs and fragmenting the overall user experience.

As Nourish scales into a multi-product ecosystem, inconsistencies will further compound, making it harder to maintain quality, efficiency, and cohesion.

## Why Pulse?

Enter **Pulse, the Nourish design system** — a unified source of truth which aims to streamline design and development, ensuring consistency, expediting delivery and elevating the user experience across all products. Pulse will refresh the entire platform whilst efficiently incorporating accessibility, scalability and general design best practices at its core.

Pulse is guided by a cross-functional **Pulse Panel** (designers, developers and other stakeholders), as a system built collaboratively to ensure alignment and adoption as we grow.
