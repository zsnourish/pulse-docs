### Usage & Content Guidelines

<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Use when the exact date is important and typing is error-prone.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-dont">
<p><strong>✗ Don't</strong> — Avoid use for relative dates (e.g. last week); use a dropdown instead.</p>
</div>
