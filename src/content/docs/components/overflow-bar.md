## Usage & Content Guidelines

<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Use when there are more than 5 items in a navigation list to keep the UI tidy.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Place overflow controls at the bottom of item lists.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Ensure the most important items are always visible and not hidden in the overflow.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-dont">
<p><strong>✗ Don't</strong> — Avoid hiding essential items that users need frequent or easy access to.</p>
</div>
