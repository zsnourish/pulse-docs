### Usage & Content Guidelines

<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Use for switching between related sections of content.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Ensure tab labels are short, clear and scoped to the same level of hierarchy.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-dont">
<p><strong>✗ Don't</strong> — Do not use for primary navigation across the site.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-dont">
<p><strong>✗ Don't</strong> — Avoid nesting tabs inside other tabs.</p>
</div>
