### Usage & Content Guidelines

<div class="ds-callout-columns">
<div class="ds-callout-col">
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Use when there are more than 5 items in a list.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Place pagination at the bottom of tables or lists.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout-col">
<div class="ds-callout ds-dont">
<p><strong>✗ Don't</strong> — Avoid using pagination for 5 or less items.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
