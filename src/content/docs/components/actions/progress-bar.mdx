**Progress component** visually indicates the completion status of a task or process. Can be determinate (shows a specific percentage) or indeterminate (e.g. animates for unknown duration).

### Usage & Content Guidelines

<div class="ds-callout-columns">
<div class="ds-callout-col">
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Use for file uploads, form completion, or any multi-step process.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Try to provide accompanying text for accessibility (e.g. '50% complete').</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout-col">
<div class="ds-callout ds-dont">
