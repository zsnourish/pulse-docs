Our **Nourish brand and product logos** are integral elements of our brand identity, and must be presented with consistency throughout our platform.

## Nourish Logo & Brand

### Logo & Variants

The Nourish logo is available in the following variants:

- Nourish Full Black
- Nourish Full Original
- Nourish Full White
- Nourish Sq Black
- Nourish Sq Original
- Nourish Sq White

### Usage Guidelines

<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Use the original, full-colour version by default where possible.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Maintain clear space. Use discretion to ensure adequate padding around the logo, preventing it from being crowded.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Resize proportionally. Always scale the logo uniformly to maintain its original proportions.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-do">
<p><strong>✓ Do</strong> — Select the correct logo variant (e.g., main brand vs. specific product) that is relevant to the UI.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-dont">
<p><strong>✗ Don't</strong> — Avoid letting the logo touch container edges. If placed within a container, the logo must never touch its edges or other nearby elements.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-dont">
<p><strong>✗ Don't</strong> — Do not crop, distort, or reconfigure the logo. Never stretch, skew, or alter the design in any way.</p>
</div>
<div class="ds-callout ds-dont">
<p><strong>✗ Don't</strong> — Avoid using the wrong logo for the context. Always use the relevant product-specific logo within that product's user interface.</p>
</div>
